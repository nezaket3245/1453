## Core Goals
1. **Local SEO Dominance** — Rank #1 for "Beylikdüzü PVC pencere", "Beylikdüzü cam balkon" and related keywords
2. **Lead Generation** — Drive phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and quote requests
3. **Product Showcase** — Present all product lines with technical specs, images, and sub-product detail pages
4. **Trust & Authority** — Show 40 years experience, 10-year warranty, Google reviews (4.9★)
5. **Lighthouse 95+** — Target near-perfect performance/SEO/accessibility scores
