# Product Context — Egepen Akçayapı

## Why This Project Exists
Egepen Akçayapı needs a modern, high-performance website to:
- Replace outdated or nonexistent online presence
- Compete in increasingly digital local home-improvement market
- Capture Google search traffic for high-intent keywords like "PVC pencere fiyatları 2026"
- Provide a product catalog that reduces phone-support burden

## Problems It Solves
1. **Discoverability** — Customers searching for PVC windows, glass balconies, or insect screens in Beylikdüzü/Istanbul need to find this dealer
2. **Trust** — Homeowners need proof of quality (Egepen brand, 40 years, warranty, reviews) before making large purchases
3. **Education** — Products are technical; customers need specifications, comparisons, and guides
4. **Lead Capture** — Visitors should easily reach the business via phone, contact form, or visiting the showroom

## How It Works
- **Homepage:** Hero slider → product grid → trust signals → testimonials → CTA
- **Product Category Pages** (6): Overview of the category with feature highlights, then links to individual sub-product detail pages
- **Sub-Product Detail Pages** (`/[category]/[slug]`): Full technical specs, images, benefits, ImageLightbox for each specific product
- **Çözüm Merkezi** (Solution Center — `/cozumler`): 13 articles with Fuse.js fuzzy search, Turkish character normalization, FeedbackWidget, TechSpecsTable for product articles
- **Tamir-Bakım** (`/tamir-bakim`): SEO content hub with 9 step-by-step how-to repair guides, symptom finder, 14 FAQ, 6 service categories
- **Blog:** SEO-driven articles about PVC windows, maintenance, energy savings
- **Contact Page:** Showroom directions with embedded map (form removed)
- **Quote Form** (teklif-al): For lead generation
- **SSS (FAQ):** 20 common questions with expandable answers
- **Projects Gallery:** Completed installation photos

## User Experience Goals
- **Fast:** Static export, WebP images, lazy loading — sub-2-second LCP
- **Mobile-First:** Most visitors come from mobile (local search → phone call flow)
- **Accessible:** Skip-to-content, keyboard navigation, ARIA labels, high contrast
- **SEO-Optimized:** Proper heading hierarchy, meta tags, structured data, sitemap
- **Trust-Building:** Social proof visible on every scroll — reviews, experience years, brand logos
- **Easy Contact:** Phone number always visible in header, floating contact bar on mobile, WhatsApp integration via QuickContactBar
